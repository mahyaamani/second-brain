# Soup Recipes

Tailored to: [Dietary Profile](/personal/dietary_profile) — no dairy, eggs, seafood, or pork. Umami-forward, Italian & Mediterranean style.

---

## Tuscan Chicken & White Bean Soup (~40 min)
- **Sauté:** diced onion, carrot, celery, garlic in olive oil
- **Brown:** chicken thighs (cubed) until golden
- **Add:** crushed tomatoes, tomato paste (1 tbsp), chicken broth, cannellini beans, kale or spinach
- **Season:** rosemary, thyme, red pepper flakes, splash of balsamic
- **Finish:** drizzle of good olive oil + fresh basil
- **Umami bombs:** tomato paste, browned chicken, broth, balsamic
- **Veggies:** onion, carrot, celery, kale/spinach, tomatoes

## Mushroom, Tomato & Miso Soup (~35 min)
- **Sauté:** sliced cremini & shiitake mushrooms until deeply browned
- **Add:** roasted garlic, diced tomatoes, vegetable or bone broth
- **Stir in:** 1 tbsp white miso paste (off heat), soy sauce splash
- **Bulk up:** cooked orzo or farro
- **Umami bombs:** mushrooms, miso, tomatoes, soy sauce
- **Veggies:** mushrooms, tomatoes, garlic

## Italian Beef & Vegetable Minestrone (~45 min)
- **Brown:** ground beef with onion & garlic
- **Add:** zucchini, green beans, carrots, diced tomatoes, tomato paste, broth
- **Toss in:** small pasta (ditalini), Kalamata olives, fresh herbs
- **Finish:** nutritional yeast sprinkle + olive oil drizzle
- **Umami bombs:** browned beef, tomato paste, olives, nutritional yeast
- **Veggies:** zucchini, green beans, carrots, tomatoes, onion
