# Meal Ideas

Tailored to: [Dietary Profile](/personal/dietary_profile) — no dairy, eggs, seafood, or pork. Umami-forward, Italian & Mediterranean style, quick-to-moderate prep.

---

## Quick Meals (Under 30 min)

### Chicken Marsala with Mushrooms (Dairy-Free)
- Pan-seared chicken thighs with cremini/porcini mushrooms
- Deglaze with Marsala wine, chicken broth, and a splash of balsamic
- Finish with olive oil instead of butter
- Serve over pasta or polenta
- **Umami bombs:** mushrooms, wine reduction, broth

### Mediterranean Beef & Tomato Skillet
- Ground beef or sliced steak with sun-dried tomatoes, olives, capers
- Season with oregano, garlic, red pepper flakes
- Toss with pasta or serve over rice
- **Umami bombs:** sun-dried tomatoes, olives, capers

### Umami Pasta Pomodoro
- Pasta with crushed San Marzano tomatoes
- Add roasted garlic, tomato paste, nutritional yeast
- Top with sautéed mushrooms and fresh basil
- **Umami bombs:** tomato paste, roasted garlic, nutritional yeast, mushrooms

### Miso-Glazed Chicken Thighs
- Marinate chicken in white miso, olive oil, garlic, lemon
- Pan-sear or broil until caramelized
- Serve with roasted vegetables or arugula salad
- **Umami bombs:** miso paste, caramelized meat

## Moderate Meals (30-60 min)

### Slow-Roasted Tomato & Mushroom Ragu with Beef
- Beef chuck or ground beef braised with tomato paste, crushed tomatoes
- Dried porcini mushrooms, red wine, rosemary, bay leaf
- Serve over pappardelle or rigatoni
- **Umami bombs:** dried porcini, tomato paste, red wine, long cooking

### Stuffed Bell Peppers (Mediterranean Style)
- Fill with ground beef/chicken, rice, roasted tomatoes, olives, herbs
- Drizzle with olive oil and balsamic reduction
- **Umami bombs:** roasted tomatoes, olives, balsamic

### Chicken Cacciatore
- Bone-in chicken braised with tomatoes, bell peppers, onions, olives
- White wine, capers, fresh herbs
- **Umami bombs:** tomatoes, olives, capers, braised chicken

### Lamb Kofta with Roasted Vegetables
- Spiced ground lamb with cumin, coriander, garlic
- Serve with roasted eggplant, tomatoes, and tahini drizzle
- **Umami bombs:** browned meat, roasted vegetables

## Umami Pantry Staples to Keep Stocked
- Dried porcini mushrooms
- Sun-dried tomatoes
- Tomato paste (double concentrated)
- Soy sauce or tamari
- White & red miso paste
- Olives (Kalamata, Castelvetrano)
- Capers
- Balsamic vinegar
- Nutritional yeast
- Good olive oil
